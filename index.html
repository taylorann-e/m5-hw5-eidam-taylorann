Hi, I’m TaylorAnn Eidam, and I’m currently a junior at the University of Florida majoring in Sports Management. I’m passionate about graphic design, social media, and storytelling, and I enjoy finding ways to blend these skills to create meaningful content. I’m especially drawn to baseball and how sports have this incredible ability to connect fans to players and teams on a personal level. My goal is to work in the creative department of a sports team, where I can focus on projects that highlight the human stories behind the game and help bring new fans into the fold, while also engaging those lifelong supporters who already feel connected.
